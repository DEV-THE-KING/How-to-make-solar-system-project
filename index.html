HOW TO MAKE SCIENCE PROJECT ( SOLAR SYSTEM )
INSTA ID- its_dev_pundhir

MATERIAL WE NEED

    1- TWO THERMOCOL SHEET ( ONE THICK AND ONE THIN )
    2- TWO BLACK COLOUR CHART
    3- ONE CELLO TAPE ( WIDTH OF TAPE ABOUT 6cm )
    4- TEN THERMOCOL BALLS OF DIFFERENT SHAPES ( FOR PLANETS,SUN AND FOR EARTH'S MOON )
    5- ONE 15 BY 15cm CARDBOARD PIECE
    6- ONE CARDBOARD CUTTER
    7- FEVICOL
    8- ONE WHITE COLOUR REEL ( DHAGA)

HOW TO MAKE

FIRST WE CUT 1/5 PART OF THIN THERMOCOL SHEET. AND DIVIDE REST OF THIN THERMOCOL SHEET EQUALLY AND PASTE BOTH OF THEM. AFTER SOME TIME STICK THICK AND TWO THIN THERMOCOL SHEET HORIZONTALLY TO EACHOTHER (TO MAKE ONE LARGE THERMOCOL SHEET AND PASTE BLACK COLOUR CHART ON IT THEN COLOUR THERMOCOL BALLS LIKE THAT THEY LOOK LIKES PLANETS AND THE SUN OK. AND PASTE WHITE COLOUR YARN FOR MAKING AXIS OF PLANETS LEFT PLACE FOR ASTEROID BELT. NOW PASTE PLANETS ON THE AXIS AND SUN ( DON'T MAKE AXIS FOR SUN ) NOW YOU HAVE THE PART OF THE THIN THERMOCOL SHEET 1/5 PART. CUT IT INTO 3 BY 2 PIECES PUT 5 TO 6 PIECES SAFE AND COLOUR OTHERS GREY AND PASTE THEM. YOU HAVE 5 TO 6 PIECES NOW CRUCH THEM TO MAKE STARSAND PASTE THEM AND PASTE MOON OF THE EARTH NOW CUT THE RINGS FOR SATURN AND PASTE IT YOUR SOLAR SYSTEM IS READY
